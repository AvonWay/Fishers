About Us

Called to Reach Souls

Founded by Dawn Moore on July 7, 2020, Fishers of Men Street Ministry began with a call from God to reach souls in the streets and hard places. We serve communities in Burlington County, NJ, including Willingboro and Camden, and reach out to areas like Kensington, PA.

Dawn Moore is dedicated to bringing hope to those who feel forgotten. Through this ministry, she leads a team that provides prayer, support, and guidance back to faith for everyone they meet.

Our mission is simple: to bring hope, prayer, and the transforming love of Jesus Christ to everyone we meet.

“Follow me, and I will make you fishers of men.”
— Matthew 4:19

Outreach & What We Do

Prayer & Worship

We believe in the power of prayer to break chains and heal hearts. We gather to worship and pray with those on the streets.

Food & Supplies

Meeting practical needs is a core part of our ministry. We provide food, clothing, and essential supplies to those in need.

Connection & Hope

We offer conversation and a listening ear, reminding everyone that they are seen, known, and loved by God.
